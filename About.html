About Mortal Kombat 1

Mortal Kombat 1 is both the 12th fighting game and 23rd installment in the Mortal Kombat series developed by NetherRealm Studios and published by Warner Bros. Games. The game was released for Windows, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and Nintendo Switch on September 19, 2023, with the premium edition having released on September 14. The game is a follow-up to Mortal Kombat 11. The game serves as a soft reboot of the franchise, the second overall reboot of the series after Mortal Kombat (2011), and follows many reimagined characters in Liu Kang's newly created timeline.
